# Getting Started with Notes

**Tags:** tutorial, getting-started  
**Pinned:** Yes

Welcome to your notes app! Here are some tips to get started:

- **Create**: Click the 'New Note' button to create a new note
- **Edit**: Click on any note card to view, then use the edit button
- **Pin**: Use the pin icon to keep important notes at the top
- **Archive**: Archive old notes to keep your workspace clean
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find notes quickly

# Recipe Ideas

### Delicious Pasta Recipe

**Ingredients:**

- 2 cups pasta
- 1 cup marinara sauce
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
- 2 cloves garlic
- Fresh basil

**Instructions:**

1. Boil pasta according to package directions
2. Heat sauce with minced garlic
3. Combine pasta and sauce
4. Top with cheese and basil
